Riela [ree-EH-lah] — from Spanish rielar,
verb meaning "to shimmer" or
"gleam faintly," derived from Latin
regillare (uncertain origin); used
poetically in Spanish.

A modern space for an ancient ritual.

Inspired by Roman thermae, Japanese
onsen, and Russian banya — reimagined
for
today's need to restore body
and mind.
